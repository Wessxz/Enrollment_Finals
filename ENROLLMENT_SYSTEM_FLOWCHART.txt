================================================================================
PHASE 1: STUDENT REGISTRATION (WEB PORTAL)
================================================================================

START
  |
  v
[Student accesses Web Portal]
  |
  v
[Student fills registration form]
  - Student ID
  - First Name, Last Name
  - Course, Year Level
  - Student Type (REGULAR/IRREGULAR)
  - Contact Info
  |
  v
[Submit Registration]
  |
  v
[System saves to database]
  - Table: students
  - status = 'PENDING'
  - student_type = 'REGULAR' or 'IRREGULAR'
  |
  v
[Display: "Registration submitted. Wait for admin verification."]
  |
  v
END (Student waits)

PHASE 2: ADMIN VERIFICATION (DESKTOP APP)
================================================================================

START
  |
  v
[Admin logs into Desktop App]
  - Username & Password

  |
  v
[Admin Dashboard loads]
  - Shows stats: Students, Users, Enrolled, Revenue
  |
  v
[Admin clicks "Manage Users" or "View Students"]
  |
  v
[System displays list of students]
  - Filter: status = 'PENDING'
  |
  v
[Admin reviews student information]
  |
  v
<Decision: Approve or Reject?>
  |
  +--- [APPROVE] ---> [Update status = 'VERIFIED']
  |                        |
  |                        v
  |                   [Student notified]
  |                        |
  |                        v
  |                   [Student can now enroll]
  |
  +--- [REJECT] ----> [Update status = 'REJECTED' or DELETE]
                           |
                           v
                      [Student notified]
                           |
                           v
                      END

PHASE 3A: REGULAR STUDENT ENROLLMENT (DESKTOP APP - REGISTRAR)
================================================================================

START
  |
  v
[Registrar logs into Desktop App]
  - Username & Password
  - Role: REGISTRAR
  |
  v
[Registrar Dashboard loads]
  - Shows stats: Students, Enrolled, Courses, Subjects
  |
  v
[Registrar clicks "Students"]
  |
  v
[Opens Student Form]
  |
  v
[Registrar selects VERIFIED student]
  - student_type = 'REGULAR'
  |
  v
[Registrar assigns Section]
  - Selects from cmbSection dropdown
  |
  v
<Check: Is section full?>
  |
  +--- [YES] ---> [Display error: "Section is FULL!"]
  |                    |
  |                    v
  |               [Select different section]
  |
  +--- [NO] ----> [Save section assignment]
                       |
                       v
                  [System auto-enrolls student]
                       |
                       v
                  [Query: Get all schedules for this section]
                  - FROM: section_schedules
                  - WHERE: section_id = selected section
                       |
                       v
                  [Create enrollment record]
                  - Table: enrollments
                  - student_id, enrollment_date, semester, academic_year
                       |
                       v
                  [For each schedule in section:]
                       |
                       v
                  [Create enrollment_details record]
                  - enrollment_id, schedule_id
                       |
                       v
                  [Display: "Student enrolled successfully!"]
                       |
                       v
                  END

PHASE 4: BILLING CREATION (DESKTOP APP - CASHIER)
================================================================================

START
  |
  v
[Cashier logs into Desktop App]
  - Username & Password
  - Role: CASHIER
  |
  v
[Cashier Dashboard loads]
  - Shows stats: Total Collected, Pending, Today's Collection
  |
  v
[Cashier clicks "Billing"]
  |
  v
[Opens Billing Form]
  |
  v
[Cashier selects Student from dropdown]
  |
  v
[System retrieves student enrollment data]
  |
  v
[System counts enrolled subjects]
  - Query: COUNT enrollment_details for this student
  |
  v
[System calculates tuition]
  - Formula: subject_count × ₱1,800
  - Display in txtTuition
  |
  v
[Cashier enters additional fees]
  - Misc Fee
  - Lab Fee
  - Other Fees
  |
  v
[Cashier clicks "Compute"]
  |
  v
[System calculates total]
  - Total = Tuition + Misc + Lab + Other
  |
  v
[Cashier selects Payment Mode]
  |
  v
<Payment Mode?>
  |
  +--- [FULL PAYMENT] ---> [Display: Total Due = Total Amount]
  |
  +--- [PARTIAL PAYMENT] --> [Calculate breakdown]
                                  |
                                  v
                             [Downpayment = ₱2,500]
                                  |
                                  v
                             [Remaining = Total - 2500]
                                  |
                                  v
                             [Per Installment = Remaining ÷ 4]
                                  |
                                  v
                             [Display breakdown]
  |
  v
[Cashier clicks "Save"]
  |
  v
[System saves to billing table]
  - student_id
  - tuition_fee
  - misc_fee
  - total_amount
  - balance = total_amount
  - payment_mode (FULL/PARTIAL)
  - status = 'UNPAID'
  |
  v
[Display: "Billing created successfully!"]
  |
  v
END


================================================================================
PHASE 5: PAYMENT PROCESSING (DESKTOP APP - CASHIER)
================================================================================

START
  |
  v
[Cashier clicks "Payment"]
  |
  v
[Opens Cashier Form]
  |
  v
[System loads students with outstanding balance]
  - Query: billing WHERE status != 'PAID' AND balance > 0
  - Display in dropdown
  |
  v
[Cashier selects Student]
  |
  v
[System displays current balance]
  - Shows: "Balance: ₱X,XXX.XX"
  |
  v
[Cashier enters payment amount]
  |
  v
<Validate: Amount > 0?>
  |
  +--- [NO] ---> [Display error: "Enter valid amount"]
  |                   |
  |                   v
  |              [Return to input]
  |
  +--- [YES] ---> [Cashier clicks "Pay"]
                       |
                       v
                  [System generates OR Number]
                  - Format: OR-YYYYMMDDHHmmss
                       |
                       v
                  [System saves to payments table]
                  - billing_id
                  - or_number
                  - amount_paid
                  - payment_date = NOW()
                  - cashier_id = logged-in cashier
                       |
                       v
                  [System updates billing table]
                  - balance = balance - amount_paid
                       |
                       v
                  <Check: balance = 0?>
                       |
                       +--- [YES] ---> [status = 'PAID']
                       |
                       +--- [NO] ----> [status = 'PARTIAL']
                       |
                       v
                  [Display: "Payment successful! OR: OR-XXXXX"]
                       |
                       v
                  [Print OR receipt (optional)]
                       |
                       v
                  END

PHASE 6: GRADE ENCODING (DESKTOP APP - FACULTY)
================================================================================

START
  |
  v
[Faculty logs into Desktop App]
  - Username & Password
  - Role: FACULTY
  |
  v
[Faculty Dashboard loads]
  - Shows stats: Active Classes, Total Students, Pending Grades
  - Shows schedule table
  |
  v
[Faculty clicks "Encode Grades"]
  |
  v
[Opens Encode Grades Form]
  |
  v
[System loads faculty's subjects into dropdown]
  - Query: schedules WHERE faculty_id = logged-in faculty
  |
  v
[Faculty selects Subject]
  |
  v
[System loads all enrolled students for this subject]
  - Display in DataGridView
  - Shows: Student ID, Name, Prelim, Midterm, Final, Status
  |
  v
[Faculty selects Term]
  - Dropdown: Prelim / Midterm / Final
  |
  v
[Faculty enters Student ID and clicks Search]
  |
  v
<Check: Student enrolled in this subject?>
  |
  +--- [NO] ---> [Display error: "Student not found in this subject"]
  |                   |
  |                   v
  |              [Return to search]
  |
  +--- [YES] ---> [Display student info]
                  - Student Name
                  - Course
                       |
                       v
                  [System loads existing grade for selected term]
                  - If Prelim selected → show prelim_grade
                  - If Midterm selected → show midterm_grade
                  - If Final selected → show final_grade
                       |
                       v
                  [Faculty enters/edits grade in txtGrade]
                       |
                       v
                  <Validate grade?>
                       |
                       +--- [Invalid] ---> [Display error: "Grade must be 65-100, INC, or DRP"]
                       |                        |
                       |                        v
                       |                   [Return to input]
                       |
                       +--- [Valid] ----> [Faculty clicks "Save Grade"]
                                               |
                                               v
                                          <Check: Grade record exists?>
                                               |
                                               +--- [YES] ---> [UPDATE grades table]
                                               |                    |
                                               |                    v
                                               |               [Set column based on term]
                                               |               - prelim_grade / midterm_grade / final_grade
                                               |                    |
                                               |                    v
                                               |               [encoded_by = faculty_id]
                                               |               [encoded_date = NOW()]
                                               |
                                               +--- [NO] ----> [INSERT into grades table]
                                                                    |
                                                                    v
                                                               [Set column based on term]
                                                                    |
                                                                    v
                                                               [encoded_by = faculty_id]
                                                               [encoded_date = NOW()]
                                               |
                                               v
                                          [Display: "Grade saved successfully!"]
                                               |
                                               v
                                          [Refresh DataGridView]
                                          - Shows updated grades for all students
                                               |
                                               v
                                          [Clear form for next student]
                                               |
                                               v
                                          <Encode more grades?>
                                               |
                                               +--- [YES] ---> [Return to search]
                                               |
                                               +--- [NO] ----> END

================================================================================
SUMMARY OF USER ROLES
================================================================================

1. STUDENT (Web Portal)
   - Register account
   - Wait for verification

2. ADMIN (Desktop App)
   - Verify student registrations
   - Manage users
   - View system statistics

3. REGISTRAR (Desktop App)
   - Manage students
   - Assign sections (REGULAR)
   - Manual enrollment (IRREGULAR)
   - Manage courses, subjects, schedules, sections

4. CASHIER (Desktop App)
   - Create billing
   - Process payments
   - Generate OR receipts
   - View payment reports

5. FACULTY (Desktop App)
   - View schedules
   - View class lists
   - Encode grades (Prelim/Midterm/Final)
   - View student masterlist
   - Export grade reports


================================================================================
DATABASE FLOW
================================================================================

students (Web Portal creates, Admin verifies)
    |
    v
enrollments (Registrar creates)
    |
    v
enrollment_details (Links students to schedules)
    |
    +---> billing (Cashier creates)
    |        |
    |        v
    |     payments (Cashier processes)
    |
    +---> grades (Faculty encodes)


================================================================================
KEY STATUS VALUES
================================================================================

students.status:
  - PENDING (after web registration)
  - VERIFIED (after admin approval)
  - REJECTED (if admin rejects)

billing.status:
  - UNPAID (initial)
  - PARTIAL (some payment made)
  - PAID (fully paid)

student_type:
  - REGULAR (auto-enroll all section subjects)
  - IRREGULAR (manual subject selection)
